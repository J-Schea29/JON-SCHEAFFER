About Me

Hi, I am an aspiring Data Scientist who not only has hands-on experience gathering agricultural data in the wine industry but also has excelled at researching tree nut data to forecast their future production capacity.

I completed the UC Davis’ Data Analytics Certificate program in December of 2021, which helped me gain valuable skills including (but not limited to) Excel, VBA, and Python. I took these skills to the next level in Cal Poly’s Master of Quantitative Economics program by learning to apply these coding skills for Machine Learning and Econometrics.

As an avid reader and lifelong learner, the intellectual stimulation provided in the field of Data Science drives me not to just look at data indifferently, but to look beyond those trends to discern the causes of the phenomenon. This is something that excites me and which my background as an Assistant Research Economist coupled with my data interpretation and problem-solving skills has well prepared me for.
